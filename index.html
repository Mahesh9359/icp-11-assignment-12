About Pune City

Pune is the second-largest city in Maharashtra and a major IT and educational hub.

Pune boasts historical landmarks like Shaniwar Wada, Aga Khan Palace, and Sinhagad Fort, making it a must-visit for history enthusiasts.

The city is also famous for its pleasant weather, bustling nightlife, and delicious Maharashtrian cuisine.
